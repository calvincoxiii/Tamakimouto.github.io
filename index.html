I'm Anthony, a programmer who also happens to do web development. I enjoy working in the system level and occasionally doing web design. I dabble in various open source projects ... sometimes prorietary but that's only on special occasion.

And this is my little spot on the internet. Feel free to stay a while and have a bit of my good wishes. If I could fax you a cookie or something, I would. I'm a fairly easy going person, don't really like to stress too much about things.

Most of what I do is in hopes of creating something useful and amusing. My favorite language is C, but I do feel pretty good writing LISP. From a web perspective, the LAMP Stack is where it's at; I've mainly been doing backend, but can venture into front end.

As a person I'm pretty self sufficient, however I don't mind, and prefer to work in teams. Lasting friendships are built on teamwork and overall productivity is increased with everyone looking out for one another.

And if you don't mind, let's be friends.
